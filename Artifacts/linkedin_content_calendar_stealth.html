Stealth guardrails — what every post must follow
✓  Share observations and data, never "we're building"
✓  Attribute insights to "research" or "experiments" — not a product
✓  Never name the vertical (promotional products) publicly
✓  End posts with a question — drives comments without pitching
✓  Link Post 01 to your Substack article; all others are standalone
✓  Post cadence: every 5–7 days, Tuesday or Wednesday morning
