Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I receive custom branded tumblers?
Standard production is 7 business days from artwork approval. Rush production is available in 3–4 business days. Blank undecorated stock may ship same day. Contact us to confirm rush availability for your specific order date.
What is the minimum order for these tumblers?
Minimum order is 25 units. Pricing starts at $10.40 per unit at 25 units and decreases to $9.40 at 1000+ units. All three decoration methods (screen print, digital print, laser engraving) are available at the same price points.
What decoration methods are available?
Three decoration options are available: Screen Print (traditional, cost-effective, up to 6 colors), Digital Print (full color, photographic quality, no color limitations), and Laser Engraving (permanent, premium look, removes powder coat to reveal stainless steel). All methods available at the same pricing. Imprint area: 2.5 inches × 3 inches.
Can I order tumblers for a corporate event or employee gifts?
Yes. These tumblers are frequently used for corporate events, employee onboarding kits, trade show giveaways, conference attendee gifts, and sales team recognition programs. Available in 5 colors. Minimum 25 units. With 7-day standard production, you can receive branded tumblers within 2 weeks of placing your order.
Is no payment required before seeing a proof?
Correct. No payment is required until you approve your virtual proof. After placing an order, you will receive an email with a digital mockup of your branded tumbler showing exactly how your logo will appear. Production only begins after your approval.
